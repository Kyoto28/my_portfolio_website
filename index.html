My name is I would like to inform you on building a professional portfolio. I had a opportunity to build a professional portfolio while I was attending ANC. You might ask yourself why I need to build a portfolio. There are several reasons to build a portfolio. This Portfolio will tell others that view your portfolio in the future your professional story. It is a way of you keeping up with your accomplishments. I was able to build this portfolio and once ANC viewed my portfolio, I was able to receive six college hours for my accomplishments. This was equivalent to two classes that I did not have to attend. I was able to use this to help speed the process of getting the needed hours to obtain my Associate of Applied Science in Advanced Manufacturing.
